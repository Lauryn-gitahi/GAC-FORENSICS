COMPUTER FORENSICS

Computer forensics is a branch of digital forensic science pertaining to evidence found in computers and digital storage media.

Uncover the missing link

As soon as you switch on a digital device, a footprint is left. The purpose of computer forensics is to uncover this trail. More data is retained on digital media that most people realise. Extracting and examining this information in a forensically-sound manner is critical to the detection and prevention of crime and often provides the evidence needed to solve a case.
